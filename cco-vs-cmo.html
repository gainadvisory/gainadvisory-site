Fractional Chief Commercial Officer vs. Chief Marketing Officer

A Chief Commercial Officer does everything a Chief Marketing Officer does, and connects it to the rest of the commercial system. You give up nothing on marketing. You gain the architecture that turns demand into durable growth.

Marketing is one part of commercialization

Modern marketing influences awareness, education, demand generation, messaging, content, and campaigns. Those functions are critical. But they are only one component of commercial success.

A company can have exceptional marketing and still struggle because:

›the ICP is wrong
›pricing is wrong
›positioning is unclear
›sales cannot tell the story
›the product is not commercially packaged
›customer success is not reinforcing expansion

Those are not marketing problems. They are commercial problems.
