Usage Notes

Do
  • Preserve the bracket weighting: [ and ] at weight 300, AI at weight 800.
  • On light backgrounds, render AI with the navy → blue vertical gradient.
  • On dark navy backgrounds, render AI with the pale-blue → light-blue gradient.
  • Pair the wordmark with "Advisory" in DM Sans 400, letter-spaced 0.22em, on every primary lockup.
  • Use Navy #0B1D33 for primary dark surfaces and Blue #1E4FA1 for any CTA, link, or signal accent.
  • Reserve the AI Green family (#1a7a5e / #6ee7c7) for agentic / AI-flavored content only.
  • Maintain clear-space around the lockup equal to the cap-height of the "G".
Don't
  • Don't render the wordmark in a single uniform weight , the bracket de-emphasis is the mark.
  • Don't recolor the AI gradient to red, orange, or yellow.
  • Don't crop or omit the "Advisory" sub-tag on the primary lockup (favicon and small-mark uses excepted).
  • Don't place the dark wordmark on backgrounds darker than #5D9BD4 , switch to the light wordmark instead.
  • Don't apply drop-shadows, outer glows, outlines, or 3D effects to the wordmark.
  • Don't substitute another sans-serif for Syne. If Syne is unavailable, leave the lockup unset and use the SVG.
  • Don't use the AI Green family for non-AI accents , it dilutes the signal.
