The reframe

Traction is not an engine.

Early revenue proves a founder is good and a market has a pulse. It does not prove the company can sell without that founder in the room. What looks like a system is often one person, carrying every deal so well the results look like a machine. Right until the day it has to run without them.

DependencyEngine

Engine vs Dependency - the one idea to remember.

The distinction

One commercial truth, not three stories.

Most companies tell one story to customers, another to investors, another to the board. A commercial architecture creates a single source of commercial truth. Marketing, sales, and product each work a facet of the same system. That is the deeper work, and it is where a company becomes both credible and investable.

The method · D2BT

Build your commercial motion the way you build a product.

Diagnose what is true. Design the motion that fits. Build, validate, and iterate it in the market. Transfer it, so the company owns it. The engine is what remains.

The intelligence behind every engagement.

It turns fragmented information into executive intelligence, so I can identify opportunities, prioritize initiatives, and decide on evidence instead of assumptions.
