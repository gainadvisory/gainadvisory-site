Built by Jason

GTMology: the private intelligence lab
behind Gain Advisory.

I built GTMology because the tools available in the market were not giving me the intelligence I needed to make better commercial decisions.

It started as a private research lab, a way to collect signals, normalize company context, analyze founder and executive commentary, and turn scattered information into better GTM judgment.

AI helps accelerate the research. GTMology helps organize the evidence. But the value comes from applying operator judgment to what the intelligence reveals.

"GTMology is not a replacement for advisory work. It is the research layer that helps me see patterns faster, ask better questions, and build more evidence-led commercial plans."

Built independently using modern AI-assisted development workflows, GTMology is also a proof point for how I think: identify a commercial problem, build the system needed to solve it, test it, refine it, and use it to make better decisions.

GTMology is used in two ways: to understand the companies I may advise, and to understand the customers, markets, and accounts those companies are trying to win. The same intelligence layer supports both.

Hiring, ATS & Operating Signals
What a company is hiring, and what it is no longer hiring, is operational context. Job descriptions, ATS metadata, and team-shape changes reveal how the commercial system is actually forming.
Executive Commentary & Public Voice
Podcast transcripts, conference appearances, founder and CEO interviews, surfaced and read for what leaders are actually thinking about.
Corporate Events & Filings

Market Synthesis & GTM Transitions
Market themes, competitive developments, and GTM-transition patterns synthesized across an entire account universe, interpretation, not just collection.
