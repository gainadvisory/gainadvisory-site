Briefing · How we work

Strategy is only useful when it becomes capability.

Gain Advisory works inside the business to diagnose what is true, design the commercial system around it, and build that system alongside your team.

The result is not another set of recommendations. It is a commercial engine the company can operate, improve, and scale.

Advice is where we start, not where we stop

Most advisors hand you a recommendation and leave. I start where they stop. I roll up my sleeves and build it with you, in the field, alongside your team. I get inside the business: your product, your company, your sales team, your positioning, the competitive landscape, and the real reasons you win and lose. I sit on live calls, sharpen the positioning in the moment, and use what the market reveals to make the next call better.

That level of involvement is deliberate. I work with only a small number of companies at a time because the work requires depth, access, and sustained attention. Clients are not buying occasional advice or a set of recommendations. They are bringing in an experienced commercial operator to help build the system with them. That depth of involvement commands a premium because the accountability and outcome are fundamentally different.

I do not just tell you what to do. I work the method with you and prove it in the market until the commercial engine runs, improves, and keeps running without depending on any single person. The deliverable is not another presentation. It is a company that knows who it serves, why it wins, and how it grows.

Why "Advisory"

Advisory describes how engagements begin. Builder describes how they end. Companies do not hire me for another opinion. They hire me because I have sat in every commercial seat and built the whole system before.

I have carried a number as an individual sales rep, led teams as a head of sales, owned the number as a Chief Revenue Officer, and run an entire business unit with full profit and loss responsibility. I have owned sales, sales operations, marketing, product, and engineering. I have built the commercial systems that took products to market and scaled the organizations behind them. That is why I can build the whole commercial system with you, not just advise one corner of it.
