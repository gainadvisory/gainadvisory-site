What Gain Advisory is (and isn't)

Advisory is where engagements begin. Builder is how they end. We build commercial capability, not recommendations, and that distinction changes everything.

Advice is where we start, not where we stop

Many engagements end with a recommendation. Ours begin there. We build the commercial capability itself, and we stay until it works. The outcome is not another presentation. It is a company that understands who it serves, why it wins, how it grows, and how that growth continues without depending on one person.
